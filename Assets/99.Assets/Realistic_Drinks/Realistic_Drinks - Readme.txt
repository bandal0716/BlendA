This Realistic Drinks pack provides a selection of realistic beverages.

Load the Realistic_Drinks.unity for an example scene, or drag your chosen drink from the Prefabs folder in the Project window into the Hierarchy window.

For accurate glass reflections, don't forget to also included the Reflection_Probe prefab in your scene at the drink's location.

Have fun!
